## 🇬🇧 English Version

### 🎮 About The Game

**AI Puzzle** is a 3x3 sliding puzzle game that uses your webcam and AI hand tracking to detect gestures. No mouse, no keyboard, no touch needed - just your hands!

### ✨ Features

- **Hand Gesture Control** - Use your hands to play the game
- **AI Hand Tracking** - Powered by MediaPipe for real-time hand detection
- **Two-Hand Capture** - Frame the puzzle with both hands, pinch to capture
- **Drag & Drop** - Pinch to pick up tiles, drag to swap positions
- **Fist Reset** - Hold a fist for 1.5 seconds to reset the puzzle
- **Timer** - Track your solving time
- **Clean UI** - Glassmorphism design with smooth animations
- **No Database** - Pure local gameplay, no sign-up required

### 🎯 How To Play

#### Phase 1: Capture
1. Show **two hands** to the camera
2. **Spread your fingers** (thumbs and index fingers apart)
3. A **green rectangle** will appear around the frame
4. **Pinch both hands** (touch thumb to index finger) to capture the puzzle

#### Phase 2: Solve
1. **Pinch** (thumb + index finger) on a tile to pick it up
2. **Drag** your hand to move the tile
3. **Release pinch** to drop the tile and swap positions
4. **Hold a fist** for 1.5 seconds to reset the puzzle
